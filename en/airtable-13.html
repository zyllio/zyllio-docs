Airtable

Integrate Airtable to combine structured records, relationships, and editorial workflows.

Why Airtable

  • Flexible schema with relational views.
  • Friendly collaboration for business teams.
  • Good balance between control and speed.

Integration pattern

  1. Model your base with stable identifiers.
  2. Map key tables to app screens and action forms.
  3. Use formulas and variables for filtered views.
  4. Validate pagination and write permissions before release.

Operational guidance

Define table ownership and data quality rules to avoid inconsistent records in production workflows.
