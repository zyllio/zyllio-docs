Custom domain

Publish your web experience on a branded domain with secure DNS and certificate configuration.

Domain setup flow

  1. Choose a production domain and verify ownership.
  2. Create DNS records according to your deployment target.
  3. Configure HTTPS certificate and redirection policy.
  4. Validate routing after deployment in all supported environments.

Go-live checklist

  • HTTPS enforced on all routes.
  • Canonical domain configured and redirects tested.
  • No mixed content warnings in browser console.
  • Domain change reflected in communication templates.

Rollback plan

Prepare a DNS rollback window and fallback hostname to minimize downtime if propagation issues occur.
