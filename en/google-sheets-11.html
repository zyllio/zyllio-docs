Google Sheets

Use Google Sheets as a lightweight backend for prototypes and operational apps.

Best-fit use cases

  • Fast proof of concept with editable tabular data.
  • Small team workflows with shared ownership.
  • Operational dashboards with frequent updates.

Connection flow

  1. Prepare sheet tabs, headers, and stable column names.
  2. Connect the source and map rows to UI components.
  3. Handle empty and malformed rows in screen logic.
  4. Validate read/write operations in Simulation.

Performance tips

Limit large unfiltered queries and prefer explicit ranges or server-side pre-aggregation for better responsiveness.
