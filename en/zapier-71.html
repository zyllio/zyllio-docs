Zapier

Connect Zyllio workflows to external tools quickly with low-code Zapier automations.

When to choose Zapier

  • Fast delivery for standard app-to-tool workflows.
  • Business team autonomy for simple automation updates.
  • Clear monitoring and retry visibility for operations.

Zap design pattern

  1. Use one clear trigger mapped to one business outcome.
  2. Add filters to prevent noisy or duplicate automation.
  3. Route failures to alert channels for rapid triage.
  4. Validate business impact from Dashboard.

Operational guidance

Version critical Zaps and document owner + fallback flow for every production automation.
