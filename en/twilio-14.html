Twilio

Integrate Twilio for SMS and communication workflows tied to business events.

Typical Twilio scenarios

  • Transactional alerts (order, booking, delivery).
  • Authentication or verification flows.
  • Customer engagement reminders and confirmations.

Integration checklist

  1. Store API credentials securely and never expose them in client views.
  2. Create backend endpoints for message dispatch.
  3. Trigger sends from validated actions in your app flow.
  4. Test delivery and failure handling in Simulation.

Compliance reminder

Respect regional messaging regulations, opt-in requirements, and quiet hours before going live.
