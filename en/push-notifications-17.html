Push Notifications

Deliver timely notifications that drive re-engagement without overwhelming users.

Notification strategy

  • Segment audiences by context, role, and action relevance.
  • Define priority levels: transactional, reminder, informational.
  • Control frequency caps to avoid notification fatigue.

Implementation flow

  1. Capture device token and consent state during onboarding.
  2. Trigger push events from backend business rules.
  3. Deep-link users to target screens after open.
  4. Measure open rate and conversion in Dashboard.

Quality controls

Test foreground and background behavior, opt-out handling, and timezone scheduling before release.
