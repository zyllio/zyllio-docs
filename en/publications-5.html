Publications

Prepare, package, and deliver your app safely to your distribution channels.

Publication preparation

  • Freeze scope and avoid last-minute functional changes.
  • Confirm all critical scenarios passed in Simulation.
  • Check branding assets, names, and legal metadata.

Release checklist

  1. Build your release artifact and verify versioning.
  2. Validate target URLs such as Custom domain when relevant.
  3. Test post-release flows: authentication, payments, and notifications.
  4. Monitor first user feedback from Dashboard.

After release

Track production issues, prioritize fixes, and schedule iterative improvements with short release cycles.
