Simulation

Test your app behavior end-to-end before publishing to users.

Why simulation matters

  • Catch UX issues before release.
  • Validate data loading, empty states, and errors.
  • Confirm navigation and business actions on realistic flows.

Test pass template

  1. Run the full primary journey from landing to final action.
  2. Test alternate branches defined in Screen sequence editor.
  3. Check data writes and reads with your target connector.
  4. Capture defects and fix them before Publications.

Release gate

Publish only after all critical scenarios pass in simulation at least once on your target device format.
