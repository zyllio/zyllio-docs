Make

Automate cross-system workflows with Make scenarios connected to your app events.

Automation opportunities

  • Trigger data sync between business tools and your app.
  • Send operational alerts to internal teams.
  • Build approval workflows for sensitive actions.

Setup checklist

  1. Define event triggers and expected outputs.
  2. Create Make scenarios with explicit error branches.
  3. Secure webhook endpoints and credentials.
  4. Validate end-to-end behavior in Simulation.

Reliability note

Use idempotent operations and retry-safe design to avoid duplicates when scenarios are replayed.
