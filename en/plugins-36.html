Plugins

Extend core capabilities with plugins to speed up delivery of business features.

When to use plugins

  • When a feature is common and already packaged.
  • When integration effort is lower than custom development.
  • When maintenance ownership is clear.

Adoption checklist

  1. Validate plugin scope against your app architecture.
  2. Test plugin behavior in Simulation.
  3. Check compatibility with your data stack and authentication flow.
  4. Review release impact in Publications.

Governance tips

Keep a short inventory of active plugins and their business purpose to avoid unused dependencies over time.
